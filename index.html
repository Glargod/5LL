Love Languages Quiz

1. How much do you enjoy receiving heartfelt compliments from your partner?

2. If your partner’s words were a warm blanket on a cold night, how often would you want to be wrapped in them?

3. When your partner does a small chore for you, like making coffee, how much does it brighten your day?

4. If someone takes time to help you with a task without being asked, how valued do you feel?

5. How much do you appreciate receiving a thoughtful gift from your partner?

6. If a gift from your partner were a treasure chest, how excited would you be to open it?

7. How much do you enjoy long, uninterrupted conversations with your partner?

8. When you and your partner share a quiet evening together, how much does it recharge you?

9. How much do you value physical gestures like hugs or holding hands from your partner?

10. If your partner’s touch were a gentle breeze, how often would you want to feel it?
